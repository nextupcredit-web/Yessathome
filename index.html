My Story

She Picked Up a Drill
& Never Looked Back

Hi, I'm Yesenia — creator behind YessAtHome, your go-to for proving a beautiful home doesn't require a contractor or a huge budget. I'm a Navy veteran, wife, and mom based in Jacksonville, Florida.

My motto? "Yess You Can." Whether it's building custom cabinetry from scratch, transforming a backyard into a resort-style patio, or finding the best budget materials — I document every step so you can do it too.

Follow along on Instagram · TikTok · YouTube · Facebook · Threads and let's build something amazing together. 🔨✨
